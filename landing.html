I'm Faezeh!

I'm a second-year PhD student at the University of California, Riverside, and I am involved in studying the universe when it was young enough that we know very little about it. My main tools are space and ground-based telescopes, and of course, some languages other than human ones (for programming, of course!).

Photography

I'm not a pro photographer, but I love capturing the beauty in everyday moments , especially nature. I recently got into night sky photography, hoping to capture the stunning night sky and the passage of time!

Pottery

I used to do pottery to satisfy my urge to create, and I've learned that you need to be patient with it. There's a unique joy that comes from working with clay and slowly bringing your creations to life (I created the piece on the left named "Rostam.").

Cooking

As a Persian woman, I can say that the love for finding new recipes and making amazing meals is in my blood! I enjoy trying different foods from all over the world and making them my way. And my Italian spirit makes me put lots of garlic in everything! (Believe me, it works!).

Painting

Painting's always been a hobby for me, but I never got serious about it. A few months into my PhD, I decided to make it a regular thing. I started using soft and oil pastels to chill out when my brain feels like it's about to explode!
